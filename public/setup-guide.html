Construct this URL (replace [CLIENT_ID] with your Client ID):

  1. Paste this into your browser's address bar

  3. Authorize the requested permissions (chat read/edit only)
  4. After redirect, copy the access_token from the URL fragment
  5. Add oauth: prefix (e.g., oauth:abcdef123456)
