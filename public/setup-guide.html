🎯 Step 1: Create a Twitch Bot Account

Create a separate Twitch account for your bot at twitch.tv/signup. Name it something like YourChannelBot.

⚠️ Important:

Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on this account under Security Settings. Twitch requires this for OAuth token generation.

🔑 Step 2: Register Your App

  1. Go to Twitch Developer Console
  2. Click "Applications" → "Register Your Application"
  3. Fill in:
    • Name: ChatWave Bot (or your custom name)
    • OAuth Redirect URL: http://localhost
    • Category: Select "Chat Bot"
  4. Click "Register" and note your Client ID

🔐 Step 3: Generate OAuth Token

Construct this URL (replace [CLIENT_ID] with your Client ID):

https://id.twitch.tv/oauth2/authorize?response_type=token&client_id=[CLIENT_ID]&redirect_uri=http://localhost&scope=chat:read+chat:edit
  1. Paste this into your browser's address bar
  2. Log in with your bot account when prompted
  3. Authorize the requested permissions (chat read/edit only)
  4. After redirect, copy the access_token from the URL fragment
  5. Add oauth: prefix (e.g., oauth:abcdef123456)

📺 Step 4: Configure ChatWave

In the ChatWave configuration page:

  • Twitch Username: Your bot account name
  • OAuth Token: The oauth:... token you generated
  • Channel Name: Your main channel (without #)

🛡️ Security Notes

Never share your OAuth token! If compromised:

  • Revoke it at Twitch Connections
  • Generate a new token following the same steps
